Privacy Policy - climate-misinfo-research

This Privacy Policy describes how climate-misinfo-research ("we", "our") collects and uses information for academic research related to climate misinformation and content analysis.

1. Purpose

This app is used only for academic research related to climate misinformation and content analysis.

2. Data we collect

  • We may access limited public profile information required for API testing (e.g., basic user ID and name) when generating a test access token.
  • We do not collect private messages, friends list, or sensitive personal data.

3. How we use data

  • For research dataset creation and analysis of publicly available content and associated metadata.
  • For evaluation of platform policy compliance and misinformation patterns.

4. Data storage and retention

  • Any collected data is stored securely and used only for research purposes.
  • Data is retained only as long as needed for the research project and then deleted.

5. Sharing

  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.
  • Research outputs are reported in aggregated/anonymized form.

6. Changes

We may update this policy as needed for research and compliance.
